## COMPOSANTS

### Buttons
- **Primary**: `bg-primary` (#A335C9), h-44px, radius 10px
- **Secondary**: `bg-secondary` (#1780E8)
- **Outline**: border `primary`, hover 15% bg
- **Ghost**: hover `bg-hover`
- **Destructive**: `bg-destructive` (#FF4D4F)

### Cards
- Background: `--card` (#121214)
- Border: 1px solid `--border`
- Radius: 12px, Padding: 28-32px

### Inputs
- Background: `--surface` (#151518)
- Focus: border `--secondary`, ring
- Height: 44px

### Badges
- Success/Warning/Error/Primary/Secondary variants
- 20% bg opacity, 30% border opacity
